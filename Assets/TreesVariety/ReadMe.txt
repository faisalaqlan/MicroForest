================Trees Variety===============
Requires Unity 2019.1 or higher.
==============================

This pack includes 48 trees (summer-autumn), 11 bonus plants + additional bonuses:
- 8 birch;
- 4 maple;
- 8 oak;
- 5 pine;
- 5 spruce;
- 4 crimean pine;
- 3 cherry plum based trees;
- 3 cypress;
- 3 linden based trees;
- 3 sakura trees;
- 2 Stankevich pine based trees.
(demo scenes for all)

Bonus 1:
Plants:
- 3 rose bushes;
- 1 beet plant;
- 1 cacao based tree;
- 2 cotton based plants;
- 2 grape plants;
- 1 potato based plant;
- 1 wheat plant.

Bonus 2:
- all source tree textures (leaf, bark);
- leaves particle effects;
- 8192x4096 clear sky skybox;
- simple dynamic clouds system (particle based);
- 5 simple bushes;
- 8 simple grass textures;
- 6 ground textures;
- Demo scenes atmospheric audio samples.
